Frugality scoring dashboard — information and guide

Efficiency

Performance-to-energy ratio

Traditional efficiency maximises the performance-to-energy ratio with little regard for context of use or real-world objectives.

Frugality

Context-aware sustainability

Frugality is context-specific — it questions application objectives and leads to more sustainable, realistic, and user-centric designs.

How the score is computed

Frugality score

A Mamdani fuzzy inference system (FIS) formalises the human reasoning process: membership functions capture linguistic uncertainty in "low", "medium", and "high" performance and energy consumption, and a rule base maps those to an interpretable score out of 100.
